Dhudhsagar Falls

The falls are located in the Bhagwan Mahaveer Sanctuary and Mollem National Park among the Western Ghats.

Baga Beach

Baga offers a wide range of water sports and is one of the most visited beaches in North Goa. Baga derived its name from the 'Baga Creek' which flows into the Arabian Sea.

Chapora Fort

The Chapora fort sits on a prominent position which commands views in all directions. It also has steep slopes on all sides.the outline of higher slopes.
